in collaboration with Suresh K Lodha, Ryan Brounley, and Karthik Thota.

Description: This visualization provides a side-by-side comparison of Chicago and New York City. There are several variables used to compare the two such as: population, income, life expectancy, and crime. A slider tool provides a direct comparison between areas in Chicago and New York City for a selected range for each metric.
