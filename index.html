Urban Displacement in Santa Cruz County

Story Line

The city of Santa Cruz and other parts of the Santa Cruz county has been known to be one of the most expensive places to live in of all of California. Home to many locals as well as UC Santa Cruz students and faculty; but with Santa Cruz being a vacation destination and neighboring the world famous Silicon Valley, the cost of living has only continued to increase each year. The following is a visual of keys factors that largely impact the communities within the County of Santa Cruz, ultimately resulting is various forms of Urban Displacement and Gentrification.
